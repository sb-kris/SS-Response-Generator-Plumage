What it does

Real responses, in minutes.

Four moves, end to end — pulled survey to populated dashboard.

Pulls any SurveySparrow survey

Questions, scales, choice IDs, custom variables — fetched directly via the SS API.

Synthesizes personas

Country, language, sentiment archetype, persona variables — all controllable, all believable.

Generates responses per persona

One LLM call per respondent — your choice of model — ratings and free-text in their actual voice.

Pushes back to SurveySparrow

Confetti, a celebration chime, and your dashboard is live. CogniVue lights up. Demo's ready.

Pro tips

Lessons from the field.

🧪

Test small first. Generate 20–50 to sanity-check tone before you commit to 500.

🌍

Match the geography. Use country filters that mirror your prospect's customer base.

⚖️

Mix sentiment realistically. 70 / 20 / 10 promoter / passive / detractor beats all-glowing every time.

✉️

Tie variables to personas. customer_fn, customer_email make NPS comments feel personal.

🧩

Pair with Weave. End-to-end vertical demos in one afternoon.

🎧

Press play and let it run. Big batches finish fastest when you stop watching.
